This Calculator gives one possible solution to reach a board situation that can be cleared with a Warlock Defile Spell.
It takes the current board status, and outputs the attack steps to reach a Defile-Clearable situation.

This Calculator takes Taunt, Divine Shield, and Reborn into account.
The Left Board takes user input, and uses E1 to E7 to represent up to 7 Enemies minion and F1 to F7 to represent up to 7 Families.
